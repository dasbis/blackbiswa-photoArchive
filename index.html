Notes on this archive

Images live only in this archive — a lock icon on upload keeps casual visitors from adding or removing frames, right-click and drag are switched off on every photograph, and a faint tiled mark sits over each image. None of this makes a picture un-copyable — a determined viewer can always screenshot a screen — it simply removes the one-click ways of lifting a file. Treat it as a deterrent, not a vault.
